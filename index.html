The Most Important Thing In Life:

FAMILY

Never turn your back on family!

Never take anything for granted!

Always pick up when your mother calls

Always pick up when your father calls

No one loves you like your parents

Never cut off your Parents.

We only have one mother and we only have one father in life

Some people don't have a mother anymore

Some people don't have a father anymore

Some people don't have either of their parents alive anymore

Be grateful if you have two parents that are still alive.

Nothing is certain in life, except death. Tell your family you love them while they are alive.

No one lives forever but the legacy and impact they can have can last forever!!!
